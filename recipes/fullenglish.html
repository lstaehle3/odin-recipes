Full English Breakfast


Description:

This is a traditional full breakfast like what can be found in England. It is not very good for you, but it is popular.


Ingredients:

  • Sausage links (traditionally Scottish Bangers)
  • Tomatoes
  • Mushrooms
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Blood Pudding (don't worry; it's a form of sausage)
  • Baked Beans

Steps:

  1. Cook sausage.
  2. Slice up and fry tomatoes.
  3. Fry mushrooms.
  4. Fry eggs.
  5. Fry bacon.
  6. idk blood pudding
  7. Heat up baked beans.
  8. Put on a plate.
