Breakfast Casserole


Description:

This is a tasty and easy-to-make casserole that uses eggs, potatoes, cheese, milk, and sausage.


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 8 eggs
  • 2 lbs ground breakfast sausage
  • 1 bag frozen hashbrowns
  • 1 cup shredded cheese


Steps:

  1. Cook sausage.
  2. Drain sausage.
  3. Cook potatoes in the sausage grease left in the pan; season to taste.
  4. Mix sausage, potatoes, and cheese in 12x9 inch casserole dish. Make sure it is level.
  5. Add milk and eggs to bowl and whisk. Season to taste.
  6. Pour mixture into the casserole dish.
  7. Preheat over to 350 degrees and bake for 1 hour.
