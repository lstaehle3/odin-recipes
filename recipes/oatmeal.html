Oatmeal


Description:

This is a bowl of extra thich rolled oats. It's very important to not use instant oatmeal. We're going to add extra stuff to ours.


Ingredients:

  • Bob's Red Mill Extra Thich Rolled Oats
  • Peanut Butter
  • Honey
  • Oranges
  • Cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Bring two cups of water to boil.
  2. Add one cup of oats to water.
  3. Bring down to medium-low heat.
  4. Cook for ~15 minutes or until water is almost entirely gone.
  5. Add one orange, two tablespoons of cinnamon, and two tablespoons peanut butter to hot oatmeal.
  6. Stir well.
  7. Enjoy.
